I'm a first-year PhD student at the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department of the University of California, San Diego. My research advisor is Professor Jorge Cortés. I received my B.Sc in Mathematics and my B.Sc in Engineering Physics from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC-CFIS). From September 2019 to March 2020 I was a visiting scholar at the University of California, San Diego. My research interests lie in the intersection of control theory, optimization and network systems.
